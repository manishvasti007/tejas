Subjects Offered

Our Commerce curriculum combines business knowledge with modern technology, giving students the skills required for today's competitive world.

Business Studies

Learn the fundamentals of business organizations, management, marketing, entrepreneurship, leadership, and business operations. This subject develops decision-making and management skills that are valuable in every profession.

Fundamentals of E-Business

Explore how businesses operate in the digital world. Students learn about online business models, digital transactions, internet-based business operations, and the growing role of technology in commerce.

E-Commerce

Understand the exciting world of online shopping, digital payments, electronic marketing, customer relationship management, and secure online business practices.

Computer Science

Develop practical computer skills, programming knowledge, digital literacy, and software application skills that are essential in modern business environments.
